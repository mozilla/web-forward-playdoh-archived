WebFWD Projects

The projects below are all part of WebFWD. Some of them are part of our Fellow program, some are Bootcampers and some are Friends of WebFWD. Together they are the reason why we created WebFWD. Each of them represents a great idea, amazing execution and an awesome team. Check the out. And apply if you want to be part of WebFWD.

Fellows

The Project: CASH Music

The Idea: CASH Music is a nonprofit organization focussed on artist sustainability through education and open technology. We’ve worked directly with artists and independent labels to build a free and open platform that can be downloaded and used to promote, distribute, and sell music.

The Team: Jesse von Doom, Maggie Vail and Jonathan “Duke” Leto

Learn more at CASHMusic.org

The Project: Meemoo

The Idea: Meemoo is a web application that encourages remixing and experimentation with web media. A Meemoo composition is a graph of modules and the wires that connect them. Modules are HTML pages that can live anywhere online, using any web technology. Wires represent how the modules send messages to each other. Compositions will be sharable on a community site. All compositions and modules will be forkable in the open source spirit of creative collaboration.

The Team: Forest Oliphant

Learn more at Meemoo.org

The Project: OpenPhoto

The Idea: OpenPhoto is an Open Source photo sharing platform which gives users complete ownership and portability of their photos. Users’ photos, tags and comments are seamlessly stored into personally owned cloud services owned by themselves. Whatever they want to do or wherever they want to go their photos are free to go with them.

The Team: Jaisen Mathai, Patrick Santana, Patrick Boos, Florian Menevis, Preet Jassi, Klaus Komenda, Kitt Hodsden, Peter Sankauskas, John Fabrizio and Gareth Greenaway

Learn more at TheOpenPhotoProject.com

The Project: Synbiota

The Idea: Synbiota is building a bio-lab in a browser. Synthetic Biology promises to revolutionize the world in ways we have yet to imagine. Synbiota is working towards this revolution by providing powerful, accessible and open tools for synthetic biology design to the world.

The Team: Diva Tommei, Mason Edwards, Connor Dickie, Justin Pahara

Learn more at Synbiota.com

The Project: Verese

The Idea: Verese keeps track of your debt and your credit among people in your social circle. Built from day one as a mobile web app, Verese mixes in a fresh interface for sparkling user experience, with some the best tools and standards of the web development world such as jQuery Mobile, Django and HTML 5. Using the open API you can make transactions from your application.

The Team: Pierros Papadeas, Giorgos Logiotatidis

Learn more at Verese.net
