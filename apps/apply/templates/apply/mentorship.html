Web FWD Mentors

We've carefully chosen an awesome and diverse panel of mentors to provide practical advice as you turn your ideas into products that will push the Web forward. These are some of the most brilliant minds on the Web today, and they're here for you. Teams will spend time with mentors each week during Web FWD. We will continue to add mentors throughout the program, so check back often for new additions.

Mentors

  • Dion Almaer VP Mobile Architecture, Walmart.com

    Dion is a technologist that loves to code, and enjoys wearing multiple hats. He loves community (FunctionSource.com, Ajaxian.com, TheServerSide.com), and building products (at Set Direction, Palm, Mozilla, Google, and beyond).

  • Mitchell Baker Chairwoman, Mozilla Foundation

    Mitchell is responsible for setting the direction and scope of the Mozilla Foundation and its activities. She leads a worldwide organization of employees and volunteers committed to an open, innovative Web and the infinite possibilities it presents.

  • Garrett Camp Founder & CEO, StumbleUpon

    Garrett has guided StumbleUpon's development since 2001, from inception to more than 15 million registered users. StumbleUpon is now the largest personalized content discovery engine on the web.

  • Shaherose Charania CEO/Co-Founder, Women 2.0 & Founder Labs

    Mobile startup junkie, love building things from scratch.

  • Marco DeMiroz Venture Investor

    Marco loves working with those unique entrepreneurs who want to change the world!

  • Kyle Doherty Senior Associate, Morgenthaler Ventures

    Kyle primarily focuses on investments in the consumer internet sector on the Information Technology team. Previously he was a Research Associate at Morgan Stanley.

  • Brendan Eich CTO, Mozilla

    Created JavaScript, co-founded the mozilla.org project, helped launch the award winning Firefox Web browser and Thunderbird e-mail client. He is dedicated to driving innovation in Internet technology.

  • Jane Finette Director of User Engagement, Mozilla Firefox

    Jane leads global user engagement and acquisition for Mozilla Firefox. Jane held various positions at eBay, Sotheby's and Channel Advisor, crafting her art of marketing over the past 15 years.
