What is WebFWD?

WebFWD (Web Forward) is the new Innovation Accelerator from Mozilla. It's a chance for us to work with builders like you, and for you to work with us and our brilliant mentors. It's about creating open solutions that push the Web forward, together.

What We Offer

The WebFWD program comes in two versions: a 6-month Fellows program and a 4-week Bootcamp.

Both programs provide

  • An opportunity to work with the Mozilla crew with access to key Mozilla personnel
  • Mentorship sessions with leading experts and interesting people
  • Workshops on topics such as scalability, application security, marketing and community building
  • Coaching on how to apply the lean startup methodology
  • Free access to Mozilla tech tools & infrastructure
  • And, of course, lots of fun & awesomeness!

Bootcamp (4 weeks)

  • A four-week intensive program based at one of our Mozilla offices (The program will kick off in San Francisco)
  • Mozilla will provide food & housing during the program so you can focus on building toward a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) in 4 weeks
  • Designed for students and hacker-builders in the early phases of prototyping an idea
  • The code you create at Bootcamp stays open, but you are permitted to fork code into a commercial license
*Applicants currently must have a US-work permit

Fellows (6 months)

  • A six-month commitment for builders who are already on the road to a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) with the option to participate for a full twelve months
  • Regular 1:1 contact with Mozillians and possible access to Mozilla offices (Relocation and full-time commitment are not required to participate)
  • Your code stays open. Potential commercial models with appropriate public licensing (like MPL) like hosted, freemium or software/platform as a service are options, but the core product or key tech innovation should remain Open Source.
*Applicants welcome from anywhere in the world

What We Look For

Mozilla's mission is to promote openness, innovation and opportunity on the Web. Our product vision is to enable people to "discover, experience and connect on your own terms." If your project shares this vision and supports openness on the Web, WebFWD is interested in hearing from you.

How?

We'll work with teams who have bold product visions for the open Web and give you access to our tools and resources.

For projects which are early in their lifecycle we bring you into one of our offices for an intense 4-week Bootcamp. Projects which are more mature enter our 6-12 month Fellow program.

You'll work alongside the Mozilla crew and receive mentorship and advice from some of the brightest people around the Web, so you can focus on what you do best: building.

When you're done, that initial gleam of an idea will be a product ready to go out into the world.

What We Offer

  • The opportunity to work alongside the Mozilla crew
  • Access to key Mozilla personnel
  • Regular mentorship sessions with leading experts
  • Help with IT infrastructure
  • Coaching on how to run a lean project
  • Introductions to interesting people
  • The chance to co-host events
  • Food & housing during the program (for Bootcamp)
  • And, of course, lots of fun & awesomeness!

What We Look For

  • Your product fits into the interest areas
  • Your product is developed with open Web traits
  • You have at least the kernel of a product

Interest Areas

Think mobile first

Traits

  • Identity

    Put me in control of how I'm represented on the Web

  • Social

    Connect me easily with people, communities and content

  • User Mediated Data

    My data is mine; allow me to broker data exchanges on my terms

  • Personalization

    Make the Web work for me

  • Contextual Integrity

    I live my life in different contexts; preserve this on the Web

  • Read-Write Ability

    Make and keep the Web hackable

  • Core

    Ensure that the foundational technologies of the Web are competitive and foster innovation

  • Your source code is tri-licensed under the MPL, GPL and LPGL or a similar Open Source license

  • You use existing open standards where they exist and push for standards where they are missing

  • Your product is essentially "of the Web" - decentralized, interoperable, secure and privacy- & people-centric

Who?

WebFWD is open to anyone with the building blocks of a great product: hackers, builders and entrepreneurs with bold ideas for a better Web.

If you and your buddies think you have what it takes to build an amazing Web app, Mobile or Social product, apply now!

When?

We're different.

Since our admissions are rolling, you can apply at any time. For Bootcampers: We're opening the doors of our San Francisco office on August 15, 2011.

Why?

Mozilla's motivation for WebFWD is to bring even more innovation to the open Web. For you, this program provides:

  • Focus to Build

    Space, time, infrastructure, tools, food, shelter—everything you need to get to a minimum viable product and beyond

  • Learning & Growth

    Mentorship and advice from some of the brightest people around

  • Mozilla

    Direct access to Mozilla and our crew in one of the Mozilla offices and tons of fun!
