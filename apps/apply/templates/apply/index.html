What is Web FWD?

Web FWD (Forward) is the new Innovation Accelerator from Mozilla. It's a chance for us to work with product builders, and for you to work with us. It's about creating open solutions that push the Web forward, together. Interested? Keep reading.

How?

We'll bring teams with bold product visions and solutions for the Open Web into Mozilla's global MozSpaces for 4 weeks at a time, giving them access to our people, tools and resources.

During the 4-week program you will work alongside the Mozilla crew. You'll receive mentorship and advice from some of the brightest people around, allowing you to focus on what you do best: building.

When you're done, that initial gleam of an idea will be a product ready to go out into the world.

What we offer

  • Food & housing during the program
  • The opportunity to work alongside the Mozilla crew
  • Help with IT infrastructure
  • Access to key Mozilla personnel
  • Regular mentorship sessions with leading experts
  • Coaching on how to pitch your product
  • Introductions to interesting people
  • The chance to co-host events
  • And, of course, lots of fun & awesomeness!

What we look for

  • Your product fits into one of our interest areas
  • Your product is developed with the traits we care about
  • You have at least the kernel of a product

Interest Areas

Traits

  • Identity

    Put me into control of how I am represented on the Web

  • Social

    Connect me easily with people, communities, and content

  • User Mediated Data

    My data is mine, allow me to broker data exchanges under my terms

  • Personalization

    Make the Web work for me

  • Contextual Integrity

    I live my life in different contexts, preserve this on the Web

  • Read-Write Ability

    Make and keep the Web hackable

  • Core

    Ensure that foundational technologies of the Web are competitive and foster innovation

  • Your source code is tri-licensed under the MPL, GPL and LPGL Open Source licenses

  • You use existing open standards where they exist and push for standards where they are missing

  • Your product is essentially "of the Web" - decentralized, interoperable, privacy & people centric and secure

Who?

Web FWD is open to anyone with the building blocks for a great product. Hackers, Builders & Entrepreneurs with bold ideas for how to make a better Web.

If you and your buddies think you have what it takes to build an amazing product, apply now!

When?

We're different.

You can apply at any time and admissions are rolling. We will open our doors in our SF MozSpaces on August 1, 2011.

Why?

Mozilla's motivation for doing Web FWD is to bring even more innovation to the open Web. And for you, this program provides:

  • Focus to Build

    Space, time, infrastructure, tools, food, shelter-everything to get to a minimum viable product

  • Learn & Grow

    Receive mentorship and advice from some of the brightest people around

  • Mozilla

    Work alongside the Mozilla crew in one of the MozSpaces & get direct access to Mozilla
